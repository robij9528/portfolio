Direct Mail Piece

In this project we were to create a direct mail piece for the Art Gallery of Ontario. I decided to create a piece for the kids classes that the AGO had, and which would be directed to families around the area. The piece would be a scavenger hunt where kids would have to use the thumbnail provided and artist's name to find out the name of the painting. The scavenger hunt would come with a coupon for 50% off kids admission, and a pair of binoculars for them to keep and which would help them find the paintings.
